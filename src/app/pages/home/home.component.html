A Community Space by Women, for Women — Ages 18+

Inclusive, creative and purpose-driven connection in Brisbane's inner west.

🧩 Who We Are

The Workshed Inner West Inc is a non-profit community initiative based in St Lucia, Brisbane. We offer a welcoming, safe, and empowering space for women aged 18 and over — focused on inclusion, wellbeing, creativity, and connection. Our programs aim to reduce social isolation, boost resilience, and support mental, physical, and emotional health.

📍 Why It Matters

Community-based spaces like sheds significantly improve mental health, reduce isolation, and support women’s employment and confidence — especially those aged 25 to 65+.
